Ping-Pong Test

Introduction to Programming (Summer 2015)

The Ping-Pong Test is a programming test that challenges a new programmer's understanding of loops, conditionals, and variables. The challenge is that when a user is prompted to enter a number, the page displays 'ping' for every multiple of 3, 'pong' for every multiple of 5, and ping-pong for every multiple of both 3 and 5 (i.e. 15).
